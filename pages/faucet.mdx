# Receive Faucet Funds

To request funds from the ApeChain testnet faucet: 
1. Navigate to the *Faucet Section* on our [Hub Page](https://curtis.hub.caldera.xyz/)
2. Enter your wallet address
3. Click the **Request** button

After a few seconds, some testnet tokens will be deposited in your wallet!
