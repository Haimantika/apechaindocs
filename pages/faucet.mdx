# Faucets

## Caldera

To request funds from the ApeChain testnet faucet: 
1. Navigate to the *Faucet Section* on the [Hub Page](https://curtis.hub.caldera.xyz/)
2. Enter your wallet address
3. Click the **Request** button

After a few seconds, you will see a deposit of Testnet APE in your wallet!

<Callout type="info">The current limit when requesting from the Caldera faucet is 1 APE</Callout>
