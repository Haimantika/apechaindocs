# Analytics

## Allium

[Allium](https://www.allium.so/) specializes in on-chain data infrastructure. They offer a suite of products designed to help organizations efficiently analyze and manage blockchain data. Their platform provides real-time data access, enriched data schemas, and advanced alerting capabilities, making it easier for analytics teams to extract insights and monitor activities.

Allium's role in this collaboration centers around providing on-chain data infrastructure, which is critical for managing the complex data needs of ApeChain. By integrating their data solutions, Allium helps ensure that ApeChain can handle large-scale data queries efficiently, providing real-time insights and support for developers working within the ecosystem.
