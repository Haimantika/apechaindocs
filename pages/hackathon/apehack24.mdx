## Hackathon Format

<details>
<summary>What can be submitted?</summary>

There are three categories of submissions, each with their own prize tracks.

<li>New Projects</li>
<li>Existing Projects</li>
<li>Open Source Contributions</li>
</details>
<details>
<summary>Can you elaborate on the categories of submissions?</summary>

New Projects: Projects are created on the start date of the hackathon, repositories will be checked, and the team will reach out if there are questions are your project

Exisiting Projects: Projects are projects which have already been deployed on X which now have expanded to ApeChain.


Open Source Contributions create something for the community, education material, addition to the docs, sample-project, etc. 
</details>
